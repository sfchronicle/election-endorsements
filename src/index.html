Battle for the House of Representatives
There was a time when Orange County was deep red. Those days are gone, and California Democrats think the birthplace of the John Birch Society now holds the key to retaking the House.

Among all the Republicans whom Democrats are trying to knock off to retake the House, one stands out for being … different. Rep. Dana Rohrabacher is pro-marijuana legalization, thinks last year’s Charlottesville riots were staged by liberals, and is such a fan of Russia that a fellow Republican once joked that “Putin pays” him.

Democrats are enthusiastic about their prospects of defeating Republican House incumbents in California -- perhaps a bit too enthusiastic for their own good.

Republicans hoping to hold onto the House have seized on an issue that they think can help them in traditional areas that have trended bluer recently -- sanctuary status for undocumented immigrants. The GOP is turning into the resistance against the Trump resistance.

Democrats aren’t the only ones hoping to flip House seats held by the other party in 2018. Republicans desperate to hold onto the House think some California Democrats are vulnerable.
