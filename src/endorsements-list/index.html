Editorial Board Endorsements
The Chronicle has made recommendations on all statewide and city races and measures on the June 5 ballot, as well as selected regional issues. Those endorsements are made by the Editorial Board, consisting of writers and editors for the opinion pages, after researching the issues and interviewing the candidates. The writers and editors involved in the news coverage of the campaigns do not participate in endorsement decisions.
